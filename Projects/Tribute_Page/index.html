Black Cats

Superstition, Culture, Folklore

good luck...

In Japanese culture, black cats are often considered symbols of good luck, particularly for single women, as it is believed that a black cat can attract potential suitors.
In Scotland, a black cat appearing on your doorstep is believed to bring prosperity, and newlyweds are often given a black cat as a good luck charm.

...or bad luck?

In some parts of Italy, seeing a black cat lying on a sick person's bed is considered a bad omen, predicting the person’s death.
The direction a black cat crosses your path determines its effect in Germany. If a black cat crosses from left to right, it's considered bad luck. However, if it crosses from right to left, it can be seen as good luck.
